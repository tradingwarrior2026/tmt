Our Trading Rules

Follow these proven rules to trade like a professional. Emphasized in all our courses.

  • Rule 1: Risk Only 1% Per Trade

    Never risk more than 1% of your capital on a single trade to protect your account from drawdowns.

  • Rule 2: Plan Your Trade and Trade Your Plan

    Define entry, exit, stop-loss, and take-profit before entering. Stick to it‚Äîno emotions.

  • Rule 3: Master Your Mindset

    Trading is 80% psychology. Control fear and greed; focus on process over profits.

  • Rule 4: Use Stop-Losses Always

    Protect against unexpected moves. No trade without a predefined exit strategy.

  • Rule 5: Backtest Before Live Trading

    Test strategies on historical data. Only deploy what has proven edge.

  • Rule 6: Keep a Trading Journal

    Log every trade, reasons, and outcomes. Review weekly to improve.
